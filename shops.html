Art Supply Shops in Tralee

You can buy drawing materials in several shops in Tralee:

  • Art & Hobby

    Lower Castle Street, Tralee, Co Kerry. Phone: (066) 712 7761 Web: www.artnhobby.ie

  • Eason Tralee

    This is mainly a bookstore, but it also offers basic art supplies such as pencils, sketchbooks, and markers. Web: www.easons.com

These shops sell many useful supplies for beginner artists.
